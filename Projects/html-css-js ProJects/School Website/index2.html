True International Standard of Education

Global International School in Nashik is an award-winning, CBSE-affiliated school located on a 15-acre campus, established by NRI Ken Kendre to bring international educational standards to India.

WHY US?

Our History

Global International School was established in 2005 with a vision to provide world-class education rooted in global values and innovation. Starting with just a handful of students, the school quickly grew into a recognized institution, offering international curricula and fostering academic excellence, creativity, and character development.
Today, it stands as a beacon of holistic education, preparing students for success in a rapidly changing world.

Contact Us

Our Vision

At Global International School, our vision is to create a dynamic learning environment that nurtures curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. We aspire to be a center of excellence where students from diverse backgrounds are inspired to reach their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally.
We aim to prepare students not only for success in exams, but for success in life.
